With over thirty years’ experience in the compressed air industry, Southern State Compressor Strives to be Georgia’s top compressed air service provider. We believe it cannot not recognized by profit margins, but could rather be recognized by long term relationships with our valued customers.

Bobby McRae

With almost 13 years experience in compressed air Industry, Bobby has proven skills as a leader among his peers as a top service professional. Bobby brings a wealth of experience in reciprocating, rotary screw, oil free rotary, oil free reciprocating, VFD compressors, VSD compressors, Desiccant dryers, controls and all system components. Bobby's has a well established reputation for his passion for the compressed air industry.
